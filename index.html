What I have worked with

Serious games project

A team of 5 individuals got the task to create a serious game for the dermatology department of Sahlgrenska University Hospital. The aim was to target psoriasis patients and to educate them about various life choices and healthy habits in order to milder their symptoms. The result: a narrative game with mini games, in which the main character has psoriasis, but the player follows them on their daily life; an Android game.

Narrative game

As part of a university course (game design), the requirement was to create a narrative game with a focus on the MDA framework and storytelling.
